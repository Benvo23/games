How to Play

Snake Head
Snake Body
Energy Cube

1. Navigate the Grid: Use the keys WASD on your keyboard to change the snake's direction.

2. Consume Energy: Your objective is to eat the randomly appearing glowing energy cubes to grow longer and increase your score.

3. Avoid Collision: The game ends if you collide with the boundaries of the grid or crash into your own tail.
