How to Play

The Goal: The objective of FormulaTypeRace is to type the displayed words as quickly and accurately as possible.

Starting the Game: Click the "Start Race" button on the homepage to begin a new game.

Gameplay: A series of words will be displayed. The word you need to type will be highlighted. Type the word into the input box and press the "Enter" key to submit it. If you type the word correctly, the next word will be highlighted. If you make a mistake, the input box will be marked, and you can try again.

Metrics:

  • WPM (Words Per Minute): This measures your typing speed.
  • Accuracy: This shows the percentage of words you typed correctly.

Leveling: Your level increases for every 30 games you play. You can see your current level on the start screen.

Leaderboard: Your scores are saved locally. You can view the leaderboard to see your best performances, ranked by WPM.
