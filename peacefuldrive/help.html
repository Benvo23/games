Help

Welcome to Peaceful Drive, a relaxing driving game designed for unwinding. There are no objectives, no scores, just an infinite road for you to enjoy the scenery. Pick your car, choose your route (if available), and just drive!

Controls

You can use either the **W, A, S, D keys** or the **Arrow keys** to control your car:

  • **W or Up Arrow:** Accelerate / Drive Forward
  • **Double Tap W:** Locks acceleration for continuous forward movement.
  • **A or Left Arrow:** Turn Left
  • **D or Right Arrow:** Turn Right
  • **S or Down Arrow:** Slow Down / Brake
