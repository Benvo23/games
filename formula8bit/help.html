HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

W / ArrowUp:

Accelerate

S / ArrowDown:

Brake / Reverse

A / ArrowLeft:

Turn Left

D / ArrowRight:

Turn Right

OBJECTIVE

The goal is to get the fastest lap time. Select your car in the garage, choose a track, and race against the clock!

Stay on the track to keep your lap time valid. Going off-track will invalidate your current lap.
